Flight Guide

Everything you need to know about flying with Corendon VA

Getting Started

Welcome to Corendon VA! Follow this guide to start your virtual aviation career.

1. Registration & Application

Fill out the application form with your details. Our HR team will review your application within 48 hours.

2. Pilot ID & Training

Once accepted, you'll receive your Pilot ID and access to our training materials. Complete the introductory flight to begin.

3. Choosing Your First Flight

Browse our Routes page to find available flights. Start with short-haul routes to build experience.

4. Flight Reporting

Use our ACARS system or manual PIREP form to submit your flight reports. All flights are tracked for rank progression.

5. Rank Advancement

Complete flights and earn hours to climb the ranks. Check our Ranks page for requirements.

Rules & Regulations

  • Always follow ATC instructions on VATSIM or IVAO.
  • Submit PIREPs within 24 hours of landing.
  • Respect all pilots and staff members.
  • No trolling or disruptive behavior on Discord.
  • Fly at least one flight per month to stay active.
