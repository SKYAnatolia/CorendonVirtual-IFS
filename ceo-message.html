CEO Message

From the desk of our founder

Welcome to Corendon VA

Dear pilots and aviation enthusiasts,

Corendon Virtual Airlines was founded with a simple mission: to bring together flight simulation fans from around the world and provide a realistic, fun, and community-driven experience. Whether you're a beginner taking your first virtual flight or an experienced captain flying complex routes, you'll find a home here.

We believe in quality over quantity. That's why we focus on realistic operations, professional staff, and a supportive community. Our team works hard to ensure every pilot feels valued and every flight feels authentic.

Join us today and start your journey with Corendon VA. The sky is not the limit — it's just the beginning.

Fly safe,
Ahmet Kaya
Founder & CEO, Corendon Virtual Airlines
